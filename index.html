Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj (1630–1680) was a legendary Maratha warrior and the founder of the Maratha Empire, establishing Hindavi Swarajya (self-rule) against the Deccan Sultanates and the Mughal Empire. Born at Shivneri Fort, he pioneered guerrilla warfare (Ganimi Kava), built a strong naval force, and was crowned at Raigad in 1674.
